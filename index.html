I am a very shy person. I rarely express myself, but I deeply treasure my memories. I take photos to remember what I felt, who I met, and the smallest things that mattered.

Photos can never fade (thanks to god-damn technology), but my stupid brain still does. I was inspired by a conversation with someone about photography (and other crazy things), and tah-dah! Here is a place to keep those memories.

All of this is truly honest to me and to the people I have met. If you like to dig deeper, I hope you will enjoy the journey with me/ my memories.
